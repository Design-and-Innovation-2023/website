    Over the past few weeks, our team has embarked on a journey to develop a website with a focus on interactive thinking and usability testing. This experience has been both challenging and rewarding as we navigated through various stages of the project, including ideation, design, and testing. During brainstorming sessions, we noticed how diverse perspectives enriched the ideas, leading to creative and innovative solutions. The open exchange of thoughts and interactive discussions fostered a collaborative spirit, enabling us to overcome complex challenges more effectively. We were not afraid to propose unconventional ideas, knowing that they would be met with constructive feedback and evaluation. This freedom resulted in several breakthrough moments that significantly improved the project's overall direction. Ultimately, our team's collaboration was one of our greatest strengths. We embraced the diversity of skills and perspectives, understanding that each team member brought a unique set of talents to the table. Encouraging a culture of respect and openness allowed everyone to feel comfortable sharing their ideas and concerns, fostering a sense of trust and camaraderie within the team. Moving forward, we intend to build upon these learnings, improving our collaborative process while maintaining a strong focus on delivering products that meet and exceed user expectations. As a team, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to implementing our newfound knowledge in future projects.

    Team 9A: Designing computing science activities using Curio platform

    A quiz system using Curio robots allowing students to engage in an interactive learning experience regarding computing science. In a team of 3 students and a Curio robot, 2 teams compete in a battle activity. The 2 robots start at opposite ends of the playing area. Correct answers make the robot move towards the goal, while incorrect answers make it move backward. The team whose robot crosses the middle line or goes further from the starting point is the victor.
